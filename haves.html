List of DOOL Episodes I Have

This page is now out of date. Please contact me if there's anything you are interested in which isn't listed below.

This page shows a list of all the Days of our Lives episodes I have available to trade. All dates are the original US airdates. If you've been here before, please visit the updates page to easily see what's new. I do have more episodes (mainly 1996 - 1999) which I haven't yet had a chance to check though and add to this page, so if there is something you are particularly looking for, please ask.

The columns Pal DVD and NTSC DVD show which episodes are already available on DVD in these formats. Although I can transfer any episode onto DVD in any video format, this can take a while (depending on how busy I am). However, I will be able to copy the episodes marked as already on DVD much quicker. NTSC DVDs will play worldwide. For an explanation of Pal and NTSC, please see the FAQ.

If you find pal/ntsc or my chart below confusing, please just email me and I'll be happy to help.
