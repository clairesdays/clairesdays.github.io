Pal Archives

I have been archiving all episodes to DVD in PAL format.

Each DVD is recorded in SP quality setting (no noticable loss in quality compared to the tapes) and holds 3 episodes with all the commercials/ads removed. The DVDs also include all the Days of our Lives promos whenever shown during the episodes on NBC.

The archives from October 2000 - November 2004 are now complete.
