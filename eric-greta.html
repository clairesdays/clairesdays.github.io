Eric and Greta Edits

DVD edits of Eric Brady and Greta Von Amberg (as portrayed by Jensen Ackles and Julianne Morris). The edits contain all of Eric's scenes and all of Greta's scenes, not just when they are together.

I've created these DVDs, not only for existing Eric and Greta fans, but also for newer Supernatural / Jensen Ackles fans that didn't watch Days of our Lives at the time, but would now like to see his scenes. Jensen was on the show for three years before leaving during August 2000. These edits are complete and contain all the scenes from his last year at Days of our Lives.

Note: If you're new to Days, you might prefer not to read the descriptions below which contain a lot of spoilers. I'd be happy to give background information on the characters and starting storylines which might otherwise be confusing.

The edits were made direct to DVD in the highest quality mode (each DVD runs for one hour). They are in NTSC format so can be played worldwide. Unless otherwise specified the edits are made from 1st generation (original) NTSC EP tapes.

1. July 19th - August 10th 1999
July 19th is incomplete. August 10th is poor quality.

Greta wants to find out what happened to her mother, Princess Gina. Eric wants to help so they agree to go to France and look for answers. Once in Paris, they bump into Bo and Hope at a posh restaurant. After opening a safety deposit box with a key they found in Gina's compact, Eric and Greta go to Gina's chateau in the countryside. They argue with the caretaker, Kurt, who reluctantly lets them inside. He tells them that the castle is haunted. They look around while Kurt spies on them.

2. August 12th - August 30th 1999
August 27th and 30th have some bits of poor tracking.

Eric and Greta explore the castle. Greta finds a poem that Gina had written for Greta but never given her. Eric scares Greta by hiding and jumping out at her. Kurt tells them that Gina is buried in the grounds. They visit her grave. Eric says the only way they'll know Gina's body is really there is to dig it up. He calls John to help get the body exhumed. Kurt wants to get rid of Eric and Greta. There is a storm, and the lights go out. Greta is scared so goes to sleep next to Eric. After Kurt tells them they can sleep upstairs, they find clothes in Gina's bedroom and get dressed up and dance. While Greta sleeps she is visited by Princess Gina (who escaped from the castle turret where Kurt was keeping her).

3. August 30th - September 17th 1999
August 30th and 31st have some bits of poor tracking. September 7th is poor quality.

Eric can't sleep because he keeps thinking about Nicole. Greta wakes up and screams when she sees Gina, causing her to run away. The next morning, Eric and Greta share a picnic. He tells her that they've been invited to a ball. Greta tries to get a box of chocolates from Eric, ending in a "moment" but interrupted when Eric's phone rings. Returning to the castle, they find Kurt in Greta's room. John arrives at the castle. They exhume the body and the autopsy proves it is really Princess Gina's body. Eric and John comfort Greta but she doesn't believe her mother is really dead.
