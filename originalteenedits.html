Original Teen Edits

Edits of all the teens, Shawn, Belle, Chloe, Philip and Mimi. Not all of Brady's scenes are included in these unless he is with another teen character.

These are mostly edits which I made on video in Pal format. They start from early 2000 but aren't complete and so don't include all the teens' scenes until mid 2000 (probably shortly before the last blast). After that they are complete, and they continue through to about August 2001. The quality varies quite a lot so please see the individual DVDs for more information.

The edits were recorded to DVD in the highest quality mode (each DVD runs for one hour) so as not to lose any quality when transferred from tape.

The DVDs are all in PAL format (as used in the UK). Please note these DVDs are unlikely to play in the United States or other countries that use NTSC (although watching on a computer DVD-Rom should work).

Note: Although these DVD edits are all in Pal format, I am able to convert into NTSC. This would take slightly longer to do, but it is possible so if you are interested in the edits but can't play pal, please let me know.

There are 14 DVDs and these contain all the teen scenes from February 13th - May 18th 2001 which covers the period from the last Channel 5 episode to the first Zone Romantica one. Most of these scenes were taken from 2nd generation NTSC tapes and then converted into pal (as edits). Not all of Brady's scenes are included.
