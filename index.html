Days of our Lives on DVD

Welcome to Claire's Days Trading! I have a large collection of high quality DOOL episodes and couple edits available for trading with other fans. Or if you have nothing to trade, I can make you copies if you cover my expenses.

My collection has changed a lot since I last worked on this site so please do check with me if there's anything in particular that you're looking for.

I'm based in the UK but can post worldwide. The DVDs I make have no region restrictions and I will send the correct video format for playback in your country. Picture quality is very important to me so I use a separate digital multisystem converter for all video format conversions.
